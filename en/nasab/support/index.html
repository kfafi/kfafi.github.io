Frequently asked questions

What is Nasab?

A private app for building and sharing your family tree with people you choose. It is not a social network: no public feed, no discovery.

How do I reset my password?

On the sign-in screen, choose “Forgot password?” and enter your email. You'll receive a code; enter the code and your new password.

I didn't get the code.

Because the app does not verify email ownership, make sure you used an email you own and typed it correctly, and check your spam folder. If it still doesn't arrive, contact support.

How does sharing work?

Per tree: you invite someone with a link and grant a “viewer” or “editor” role, and you can revoke access at any time.

Who can see my tree?

Your tree is private by default, and living people are private by default. You control the visibility of each record.

Can I export my data?

Yes — export your tree as GEDCOM from inside the app.

How do I correct information?

Edit any record directly in the app.

What about photos?

You give Nasab a photo URL; the app stores the link, not the image file.

Account and data deletion

To delete your account from inside the app: menu → “Delete account” → confirm.

What is deleted:

  • Your account (your login).
  • Every tree you own, and all people and marriages in them.
  • All sharing grants to and from your account.

Deletion is immediate, permanent, and irreversible.

What remains: tribes you created remain as shared reference for others.

If you cannot sign in, email support@kefafi.dev from your registered email and we will delete it for you.

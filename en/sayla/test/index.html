Try Sayla — beta

Sayla is currently a closed beta on Google Play. Join the testing group to try it before launch and help us with your feedback.

Request to join Join the test Open in Google Play

How to join the testing group

The test is closed, so we add your email first. Three steps:

  1. Send us the Google account you use on the Play Store — via the “Request to join” button below — so we can add you to the tester list.
  2. Once we confirm you're added, open “Join the test,” sign in with that same email, and accept the invitation.
  3. Return to Google Play and install Sayla.

After your email is added and you accept, it can take a few minutes for the app to appear in the store.

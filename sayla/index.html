We can’t see your data — by design

Send things between your own devices.

Sayla moves files, links, and notes between your phone, tablet, laptop, and browser — through your own Google Drive. There’s no Sayla server in the middle, no account to create, and nothing for us to read.

Get in touch ↗
  • No backend Your stuff travels through your own Google Drive. We don’t run a relay and never hold a copy.
  • The narrowest permission Sayla uses Google’s drive.file scope — it can only touch the files it creates, never the rest of your Drive.
  • No ads, no tracking No analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs. Nothing phones home.
  • Yours to delete Set an auto-delete timer, remove items in the app, or wipe the folder in Drive. You hold the keys.
